Palenque




Palenque, also anciently known as Lakamha (meaning”Big Water”), was a Maya city state in southern Mexico that flourished in the 7th century.

The Mayan civilization showed some unique skills in building temples and pyramids. They also showed a complex writing system and deep astronomical knowledge.

The big mystery surrounding this fascinating (and, in some aways, advanced) civilization revolves its disappearance.

Their cities had collapsed before the arrival of the invading conquistadores. Palenque, in the state of Chiapas in southern Mexico, is just one of many mysterious monuments left behind
